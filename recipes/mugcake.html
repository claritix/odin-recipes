Chocolate Mug Cake

Credit : Lauren Allen


Description

This microwave Chocolate Mug Cake recipe will become your new obsession; it's the easiest and fastest way to make dessert for one or two.

Ingredients

  • 3 Tbsp all-purpose flour
  • 2 Tbsp granulated sugar
  • 1 Tbsp unsweetened cocoa powder
  • Â¼ teaspoon baking powder
  • dash, salt
  • 3 tablespoons milk
  • 1 tablespoons canola oil
  • 1/8 teaspoon vanilla extract
  • 1 tsp chocolate chips

Steps

  1. Spray the bottom of a microwave-safe mug or coffee cup lightly with cooking spray.
  2. Add flour, sugar, cocoa powder, baking powder, and salt and stir together.
  3. Add milk, canola oil, and vanilla extract and stir until smooth, being sure to scrape the bottom of the mug. Stir in chocolate chips or sprinkle them on top.
  4. Cook in microwave for 70-90 seconds until cake is just set, but still barely shiny on top. Allow to rest in microwave for 1 minute before consuming.
